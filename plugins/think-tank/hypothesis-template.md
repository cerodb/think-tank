# Hypothesis

## Objective

_What question are you trying to answer? What decision does this inform?_

## Current Hypothesis

_State your hypothesis clearly and specifically. It should be falsifiable._

Example: "The authentication module uses JWT tokens stored in localStorage,
making it vulnerable to XSS attacks."

## Constraints

_Boundaries for the research — what is in scope and out of scope._

- Scope: _directories, modules, or concerns to focus on_
- Out of scope: _what to ignore_
- Time budget: _max cycles or time limit_

## Success Criteria

_How will you know the hypothesis is confirmed or rejected?_

- Confirmed if: _specific evidence that would prove it true_
- Rejected if: _specific evidence that would prove it false_
- Pivot if: _conditions that suggest a revised hypothesis_
